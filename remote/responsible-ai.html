This topic concerns algorithmic systems that are responsible for important tasks in society.

  • Urkund - a system for automatically detecting plagiarism in academic essays. It works by conveniently presenting similar passages in source material and essay material to the supervisor of a text. The system never determines whether something is plagiarism on its own, but chooses "where to direct the attention of the supervisor".
