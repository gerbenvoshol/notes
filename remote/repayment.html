Repayment

Tags:
  • reminders
  • lists
  • social

Some ideas on how to repay people you are thankful for.

  • Stay in touch. Talk often.
  • Make something for them. It could be a small gift in a postcard, it could be a useful tool, it could be a work of art.
  • Invite them over for dinner or lunch.
  • Teach them something useful.
