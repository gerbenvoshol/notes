Words

Tags:
  • monologue
  • philosophy

Poisonous, toxic things.

Words can skew our minds. Words can make us think, feel, control. One should always, at all times, know where words come from. Who wrote them, and why.

They are also permanent. This, this note-taking system, is only useful because it makes words permanent. Medium. Media. It organizes, controls, skews, tells.

Where would we be without words?
