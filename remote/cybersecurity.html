Cybersecurity

Tags:

  • glossary

The topic of cybersecurity is interesting because
it applies to so many things today. It is also
a fundamental component of Distrust.

Cybersecurity grants us the choice of who to allow to understand
our data, assuming that we can trust that the key for the data
is only in the possession of the people who we want to read it.
It is the fundamental component for expressing trust
on the internet.
