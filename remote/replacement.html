Replacement

Tags:
  • glossary
  • philosophy
  • society

The act of throwing away an object, usually because it no longer fulfills its function, and putting a new object with a similar function in its place. It is strongly tied to Waste.

An alternative to Replacement is Restoration which focuses on restoring the function of the object rather than throwing it away, avoiding Waste.

Replacement is common in a world where new functional objects can be conveniently traded for money, requiring little to no effort.
