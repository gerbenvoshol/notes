Remove Patina and Soot from Brass

Tags:
  • guides
  • craft
  1. Make a paste of:
    • 1/3 Flour
    • 1/3 Salt
    • 1/3 Vinegar
  2. Cover the brass in the paste. Maybe put it in a plastic bag or wrap.
  3. Let rest for 48 hours.
  4. Clean off the paste from the brass.
  5. Sand until the brass recovers its shiny brass color.
