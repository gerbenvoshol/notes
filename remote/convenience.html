Convenience

Tags:
  • philosophy
  • glossary

A term to describe something that allows less effort to be expended in order to perform a task.

Convenience is the basis of this note-taking system.

The less time we have to use in order to do things, the more efficient we become. Spending time to develop tools that remain effective in the future may save time in the long run.

One can make their own convenient applications by educating themselves in programming. There are also big changes to be made in order to make writing software easier and faster.

Convenience is also the weapon of the distrusted.

For us, living off grid is most of all letting go of the numbing culture of convenience.

-Rekka Bell
